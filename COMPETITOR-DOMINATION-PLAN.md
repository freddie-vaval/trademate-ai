## THE 5 PROBLEMS EVERY COMPETITOR HAS

**Problem 1: Impossible to cancel**
Jobber has an F-rating with the Better Business Bureau. Reviewers describe a deliberately designed cancellation trap — password reset loops, requirement to log in to cancel, charges continue after removal of payment method. This is industry-wide behaviour.

**Problem 2: Charges during "free trials"**
Hundreds of reviews document people being charged during free trials — £424, £1,000+, £1,700. None of them get refunds unless they go public with negative reviews. This is systemic.

**Problem 3: US software, poor UK compliance**
Housecall Pro and Jobber are US companies. UK-specific requirements — VAT, Making Tax Digital (MTD), UK invoice formatting, UK payment processing — are bolt-ons or missing entirely.

**Problem 4: No AI, no innovation**
Every single competitor is a database with a calendar and a job board. Job scheduling, client records, invoicing — that's all they do. None of them have AI answering phones. None of them auto-generate social content. None of them have intelligent lead follow-up.

**Problem 5: Expensive for what you get**
Jobber charges $199/month for what is essentially a spreadsheet with a calendar. Housecall Pro charges $59-199/month for the same thing. None of the AI features competitors mention exist in any of them.
