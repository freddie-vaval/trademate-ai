## Engagement Tips

1. **Respond to comments** — If someone replies with pain, DM them personally
2. **Be helpful first** — Answer questions, share advice
3. **No hard sell** — This is discovery, not sales
4. **Ask for referrals** — "Know any other tradies who'd want to chat?"

---

## What to Look For

- Comments that describe specific pain (copy these!)
- People asking questions about the product (sign of interest)
- Anyone offering to try it (pilot customer!)
