## Discovery Script

> "Hi, I'm building something for tradespeople and I need to talk to people who actually do the job — not consultants, not agencies. Would you have 5 minutes to tell me what drives you mad about running the admin side of your business?"

**Pilot conversion:**
> "This is exactly what I'm building against. Would you want to be one of the first 10 people to try it free — and help shape what it becomes?"

## What to Track

1. Exact words they use for pain (copy gold)
2. Would they pay?
3. Did they ask questions? (unprompted curiosity = strong signal)
