# TradeMate AI - Demo Video Production Script
## 60-Second Commercial

---

## TIMING & CONTENT

### HOOK (0-5 seconds)
**Visual:** Phone buzzing on van dashboard, goes to voicemail
**Audio:** "That was £800 just drove off down the road"
**VO:** None - let the moment land

---

### PROBLEM (5-15 seconds)
**Visual:** Rapid montage - tradesperson on roof, plumbing, drilling - can't answer phone
**VO:** "You're on a job. Phone rings. Another customer lost."
**Tone:** Gritty, real, not polished

---

### SOLUTION (15-30 seconds)
**Visual:** Clean product reveal - phone answered by AI, quote sent, job booked
**VO:** "Meet TradeMate AI. Your virtual assistant that answers calls, sends quotes, and books jobs — all while you get on with the work."
**Tone:** Confident, shifts from gritty to professional

---

### FEATURES (30-50 seconds)
Four 5-second cuts:

**1. 30-35s - Phone Receptionist**
- Visual: Phone rings, AI answers
- VO: "Answers every call. 24/7."
- Text: "AI Phone Receptionist"

**2. 35-40s - Quotes & Invoices**  
- Visual: Tap, quote sent, invoice paid
- VO: "Sends quotes and invoices in seconds."
- Text: "Quotes in Seconds"

**3. 40-45s - Job Scheduling**
- Visual: Calendar fills, jobs scheduled
- VO: "Books the jobs while you're on the tools."
- Text: "Smart Scheduling"

**4. 45-50s - Financial Tracking**
- Visual: Dashboard shows income, profit
- VO: "Tracks your money. Knows your profit."
- Text: "Financial Tracking"

---

### SOCIAL PROOF (50-55 seconds)
**Visual:** Bold number on screen
**VO:** "Tradespeople are recovering £2,400 a month. That's a week's wages — just from picking up the phone."
**Text:** "£2,400/month recovered"

---

### CTA (55-60 seconds)
**Visual:** Simple URL, clean
**VO:** "TradeMateAI.co.uk. First 100 get half price. Forever."
**Text:** TradeMateAI.co.uk
**Offer:** 50% off for life

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voice
- Male, 35-50, UK accent (cockney/london preferred)
- Sounds like a mate, not a corporation
- Confident, not salesy

### Music
- Build: Tense, missing calls (heartbeat)
- Solution: Uplifting, hopeful
- Features: Driving, montage pace
- CTA: Triumphant finish

### Colour Grade
- Problem: Desaturated, flat
- Solution/Features: Warm, orange highlights (trades theme)
- Contrast: Deep blacks, warm highlights

### Text Treatment
- Bold, sans-serif
- White with dark outline
- Appears on beat with VO
- Minimal - not cluttered
