## Post 4: Lessons Learned

---

**🤔 Things I learned building Trademate AI (Day 3):**

1. Tradies don't want "software"
They want more jobs and less hassle.

2. The phone is the #1 problem
Not invoicing. Not scheduling. MISSED CALLS.

3. AI-first beats AI-added
Every competitor bolted AI onto existing software.
We built it from the ground up.

4. UK-first matters
Everyone else is US-focused. The UK market is wide open.

5. Content > cold outreach
Instead of cold calling 100 tradies, we're making content they want to watch.
